I am currently a fourth-year PhD student in Information Systems at Stern School of Business , New York University. Previously, I received my BS in mathematics and MS in statistics from University of Minnesota.

My research interests broadly lie at the intersection of economic and technical aspects of technology, in particular, technology-enabled commerce. Thus far, I have sought to answer questions like (1) How does technology-enabled two-sided platform policy change affect decision-making process from both sides? (2) How to leverage fine-grained unstructured data (e.g., GIS, text, image and video data) assisted with machine learning algorithms, to understand and optimize individual decison making?
